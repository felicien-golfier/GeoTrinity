# Ability descriptions shown in the pause menu's Abilities view.
# One [Ability.Spell.X] section per ability, keyed by the AbilityTag from the ability's AssetTags.
# The section body replaces the Description field of the matching AbilityInfo entry; entries absent
# here fall back to the asset's Description. {Token} syntax: see AI/AbilityDescriptions.md.
# Lines starting with # are comments. The file is re-read on every menu open — edits show live.

[Ability.Spell.Dash]
Dash {DashDistance} units in your movement direction over {DashDuration}s.

[Ability.Spell.SimpleProjectile]
Hold to fire projectiles, each dealing {Damage} damage.

[Ability.Spell.Reload]
Restores your ammo and drops a buff pickup nearby, chosen by your remaining ammo (random only if you were out of ammo). The emptier your magazine, the stronger the buff:
{Effects:range}

[Ability.Spell.TurretLaunch]
Hold to aim, release to deploy a turret between {MinDeployDistance} and {MaxDeployDistance} units away. The turret fires at enemies and slowly drains its own life.

[Ability.Spell.RecallTurret]
Recalls all your deployed turrets. Each returning turret damages every enemy on its path back to you:
{Effects}
Blinking turrets (about to expire) deal {BlinkBonusEffect}.

[Ability.Spell.BasicBeam]
Hold to charge, release to fire a beam dealing {Damage} damage, scaled to {MinDamageMultiplier:%} (no charge) up to {MaxDamageMultiplier:%} (full charge). Release in the sweet spot for {SweetSpotDamageMultiplier:%} instead.

[Ability.Spell.MoiraBeam]
Channels a beam for {InitialDuration}s that deals {DamagePerSecond}/sec damage to enemies and heals allies for {HealPerSecond}/sec, while boosting your movement speed by {SpeedBuffEffect:%}. Absorbing your Healing Zones on the beam path extends it by {DurationPerAbsorbedZone}s, widens it, and boosts its damage and healing by {DamageAndHealBoostPerAbsorbedZone:%} per zone and {BoostPerFinishedZone:%} bonus per finished absorbed zone, up to {DamageAndHealBoostPerAbsorbedZone*MaximumZoneAbsorbed:%} boost;

[Ability.Spell.HealingZone]
Hold to aim, release to deploy a healing zone between {MinDeployDistance} and {MaxDeployDistance} units away. Allies inside are healed over time until the zone's life runs out.

[Ability.Spell.HealingAura]
Allies touching you are healed for {HealPerSecond}/sec.

[Ability.Spell.HealsYouWhatYouHeal]
You heal yourself for {SelfHealPercent:%} of every heal you provide to allies.

[Ability.Spell.SweetSpotCharge]
Your first heal starts a {ChargeDuration}s charge that records all healing you provide. Once charged, your next sweet-spot beam release deals {HealToDamageRatio:%} of the recorded healing as damage instead of its base damage, emptying the gauge.

[Ability.Spell.ShieldBurst]
Every point of damage you deal fills a gauge. At {GaugeFillThreshold} damage dealt, fire a slow shield projectile forward. It bounces off enemies, growing its shield by {EnemyBounceMultiplier:+%} per bounce, then ends by granting {ShieldAmount} shield to the first ally it touches. Passes through your own walls; bouncing off walls or terrain does not grow it.

[Ability.Spell.Mine]
Hold to aim, release to deploy a wall between {MinDeployDistance} and {MaxDeployDistance} units away. The wall blocks projectiles and slowly drains its own life.

[Ability.Spell.DetonateMine]
Fires a ray that consumes your walls on its path — each consumed wall powers the ray with {WallBoostMultiplier:%} of its base output. The ray deals {BaseDamage} damage to enemies and grants {BaseShield} shield to allies it crosses.

[Ability.Spell.SacrificeBeam]
Channels a beam that marks allies inside it: marked allies take no damage — it's split evenly between you and your alive walls instead, and every point is stored for the detonation. Channel ends after {MaxChannelDuration}s, arming the detonation automatically.

[Ability.Spell.SacrificeDetonate]
Unleashes the stored sacrifice as a ray, dealing {BaseDamage} damage plus {SacrificeValueMultiplier:%} of absorbed damage to every enemy on its path. Pressing during the channel detonates immediately.

[Ability.Spell.SpiralHellfire]
Launch a projectile spiral spree

[Ability.Spell.SpawnPillar]
Telegraphs a danger zone that explodes after a delay, dealing fatal damage to anyone caught inside.

[Ability.Spell.DevastatingWave]
Boss teleports to center and fires an expanding radial wave of projectiles outward.

[Ability.Spell.ProjectileToAllPlayers]
Fires a projectile toward each player simultaneously.
